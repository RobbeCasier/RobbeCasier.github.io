Wwise integration Unity
University Project
Sound design and integrate sound through Wwise in Unity. Spent around 16-20 hours on it.
Most sounds for the enemy were made with a brush.
For the spur I found an item in my attic that displays the days in a month by flipping the top part. Shaking this item makes a similar sound.
The music changes when entering/exiting the town and when the character enters/exit battle.
The footsteps check for the dominant layer from the terrain data.

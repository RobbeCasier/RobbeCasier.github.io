Zombie Survivla Game AI
University Project
The goal of this project was to let the AI survive as long as possible against a hord of zombies. The AI has to walk through the map and search for resources it could use to achieve this. These resources spawn only in houses, but the AI doesn't know more than what is in it's FOV.
In order to be able to search the whole map, I let the AI to 4 points on the map and while it goes to those points it collects where houses are and searches through them. With this knowledge, the AI doesn't has to rely on the 4 points to investigate houses after all 4 points have been reached. All other decisions where made with a behavior tree.

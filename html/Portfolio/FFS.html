Forest Fire Simulation
University Project
Research topic, collaboration with KU Leuven, University in Leuven, Belgium. For people to understand the working of the hearts electrical waves.
Main goal was to get a working forest fire simulation that can be realtime edited. This includes different parameters and making a tool for making rivers and trenches to stop the fire.

VR Sniper Trainer
University/Client Project
A project I had to make with 5 other people through our University for a client.
Unfortunatly I couldn't retreive the files I worked on from the Perforce Server. This was my first project I really got into Unity, C# and VR. The things I had to do in this project, where: - The scope functionality of the rifle (Zoom, Windage Dial and Elevation Dial) - A range binocular and marking the targets on the screen of the binocular when in screen or with an arrow against the sides of the screen to indicate the direction - Tutorial screens, these screens were world screens with a marker and when you looked at it they would slide open and display tutorial text

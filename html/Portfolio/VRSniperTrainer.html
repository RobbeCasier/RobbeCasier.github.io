VR Sniper Trainer
University/Client Project
In case the video doesn't work, link here
A project I had to make with 5 other people through our University for a client as a prototype. With a physical controller constructed from an airsoft with some 3D-printed parts to store one Oculus controller and an arduino chip for the bluetooth connection.
The things I had to do in this project, where:
- The scope functionality of the rifle (Zoom, Windage Dial and Elevation Dial)
- A range binocular and marking the targets on the screen of the binocular when in screen or with an arrow against the sides of the screen to indicate the direction
- Tutorial screens, these screens were world screens with a marker and when you looked at it they would slide open and display tutorial text
- Some smaller things.
For every target in the world, a position on the screen of the range finder will be displayed. A red dot should appear to make it easier to find the target.
When the user rotates the physical dial, a change in the scope should happen. This has to happen for 3 dials.
